## Blood in bathroom Dec 24 2021

The first time I was asked to clean up blood I was very upset. I had seen a video that only salaried personal resolve this kind of cleanup. I was even more angered when the managers were upset with me that I did not want to do it. It is my understanding Igor cleaned it up. That is a concern as well because I know regardless of who did it nobody used any PPE. I know this because I was wandering around trying to figure out what the PPE was and who I could ask about what to do. Before I could do that the blood was cleaned up and they missed a spot. The bucket used to clean the blood was left by the restrooms. The water was not changed and the mop head was not changed. In fact the water was not changed until about Dec 27th at night because that would have been the graveyard shift that would go until Tuesday the 28th. The night person had to change the water and the mop head. From what I heard the blood was still visible as the bucket was being emptied into the utility sink.

## Doing work that aggravated pre-existing injuries

I had mentioned to my General Manager that I have feet and back issues. The bags have to be thrown into the back of a large semi-trailer and to throw them you have to twist. I had a Physical Therapist at OHSU write a letter with things like lifting a certain amount of weight, twisting, and using a pallet jack as things to avoid so I did not injure myself. Which they understood after I had to take several days off without pay after I called Sedgwick.

## Spraying carts during covid

My first week during covid, I was told to spray the carts with a disinfectant. At first I used spray bottles with the words Greaselift on it. Later I was told by one of the Leads Amy that is a 5,000 fine if we use the wrong bottle. The bottle was supposed to have a color similar to the 4th picture. So we migrated to a backpack sprayer but the employees are careless and incompetent and eventually it was broken. Then we didn't spray the carts for a while because the GM didn't want to buy another sprayer. I suggested the 10 dollar HDX and we went through 4 of them because people would leave them outside and they would get stolen.

## Collecting Trash

At first I was just collecting trash. I was told to go out into the parking lot and get all the trash. To me that meant all of it so I walk the entire parking lot. When I first started I would keep track of how much I collected. I wanted to make sure I was doing a good job. I found it funny that most of the time if I left the bucket full it would have the same items in it the next day. I quickly learned that the afternoon and night shift doesn't collect trash.
